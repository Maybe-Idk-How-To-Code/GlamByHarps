Professional Makeup Artistry for Every Occasion

Hello, my name is Harps. I’m a London-based makeup artist with a focus on South Asian bridal hair and makeup. For me, makeup isn’t just a skill it’s a way to celebrate and enhance your natural beauty. Every face has a story, and I love helping my clients feel confident, radiant, and truly themselves, whether it’s for a wedding, a film set, or a creative photoshoot.

Bridal Services

Expert South Asian Bridal Makeup & Hair, Harps provides personalised bridal looks that highlight your natural beauty while capturing the elegance and vibrancy of your special day. From traditional South Asian bridal styles to modern interpretations, she ensures your look is flawless, long-lasting, and picture-perfect.

Bridal Trial

Your bridal trial is where the vision comes to life. Harps works closely with you to design and refine your perfect wedding look, carefully considering your outfit, jewellery, skin type, and personal style. This session allows you to test products, adjust details, and ensure every element feels authentic and flawless — giving you complete confidence ahead of your big day.

Event & Special Occasion Makeup

Glam for Parties, Photoshoots & Celebrations, From fashion shoots to weddings, red carpet events, or photoshoots, Harps crafts bespoke looks that complement your style and outfit. She ensures you feel confident, radiant, and camera-ready for any event.

1 to 1 Lesson

Harps’ personalised 1-to-1 makeup lessons are designed to help you master techniques suited to your own features, skin type, and lifestyle. Whether you want to perfect an everyday routine or learn how to create a glamorous evening look, she provides hands-on guidance, product recommendations, and professional tips so you leave feeling skilled, confident, and empowered.

Destination Wedding Makeup

Planning a wedding abroad requires expertise and adaptability. Harps offers destination bridal services tailored to different climates, lighting conditions, and cultural settings. Using long-wear, high-performance products and techniques, she ensures your makeup and hair remain flawless from ceremony to reception — wherever in the world your celebration takes place.

Guest Hair and Makeup

For wedding guests and family members, Harps creates polished, elegant looks that enhance natural features while complementing outfits and the overall event aesthetic. Whether you prefer soft glam, classic sophistication, or full glam, she ensures your hair and makeup last throughout the celebrations so you can enjoy the day feeling confident and camera-ready
